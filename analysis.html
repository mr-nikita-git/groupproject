Why You Can't Afford a House Anymore

Government Policies
Right to Buy reduced public housing stock drastically. Help to Buy inflated demand, driving prices up while supply stayed flat. Short-term vision, long-term mess.
Supply & Demand
New builds lag far behind population growth. 31% fewer rentals since 2019, double-digit rent hikes in many regions. Demand crushes supply = prices rocket.
Economic Pressures
Inflation’s rising, wages aren’t. House prices now equal 8.6 years of income vs 4.4 in 1999. First-time buyers? Good luck.

Let me make it simple for you: If you're not already in the game, you're late. The UK housing market is a rigged system of debt, inflated values, and shrinking access. While the elites buy their third homes, you're out here hustling for a shoebox flat. Either you adapt and play smarter, or you rent forever. Your move.
