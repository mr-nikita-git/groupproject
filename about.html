What Are We

The housing market tells more than just a story of prices—it reveals the values and priorities of a generation. Our message isn’t to provoke outrage or assign blame, but to observe, reflect, and present the truth as it stands.

The UK’s current housing situation is not the result of a single event, but of decades of political inertia, economic pressure, and shifting societal expectations. We believe that clarity leads to awareness, and awareness is the first step toward change.

This project aims to illuminate how housing transformed from a basic need into a benchmark of privilege—offering a lens into the choices, struggles, and consequences of a nation in transition.
